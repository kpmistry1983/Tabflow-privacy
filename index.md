

# Tabflow Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

## Data Collection
Tabflow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

## What We Access
Tabflow reads tab titles and URLs solely to classify and group tabs by topic. This data never leaves your browser.

## Local Storage
Tabflow uses Chrome's local storage to save your sessions and settings on your own device. This data is never sent to any server.

## Third Parties
Tabflow does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.
